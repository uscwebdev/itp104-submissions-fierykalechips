What is a cat?

Why, it is a wonderful little creature. With four slinky limbs, a round observant face, and a physics defying body, a cat seems to be a perfect companion for your home. They offer amazing rates of mood improvement, simply just by existing in your home or environment!


Cats are often thought of as the opposite of dogs, who are far more energetic and loyal, whereas a cat is independent and nonchalant. Nevertheless, a cat is a wonderful addition to anyone's life, yours included!


Skills

Cats have a wide range of skills and hobbies. They often act on their own wants and needs, such as sitting (pictured on the right) or sleeping for 18 hours a day. In the rare occasion that a cat is awake and active, they enjoy leisure activities such as looking out the window wistfully and meowing bloody murder for the next meal. Though the listed skills seem simple, cats have ulterior motives behind all of their actions, such as destroy, manipulate, or lounge.


Cats are very intelligent animals and definitely have their own feelings independent of their humans. Cats can often feel a sense of recklessness, in which they seemingly do things to purposely be a nuissance. This can be mild, such as knocking things off the counter, or more severe, like scratching up the furniture.


Interacting with Cats

Cats seem pretty independent based on the description thus far, but that does not mean that we as humans should avoid contact with them. There are many ways to entertain felines and interact with them smoothly. Many cats enjoy the occasional head and chin rub, and will close their eyes blissfully when enjoying the service. Outside of the tame interactions, cats enjoy more aggressive and energetic interactions too.


Most cats have an innate instinct to hunt, even though they live peacefully in a home where their every meal is provided for. To supplement this enrichment, one can use "cat toys" to simulate a small critter for your cat to chase around and catch. Another instinct leftover from their ancestral wild life would be their intense love for butt pats. They love being absolutely bonked on their butts, it is like a nice massage for them!
