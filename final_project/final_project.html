About Me

I graduated from Glendale High School in 2020 during the pandemic and went on to Pasadena City College where I prepared to transfer to a 4-year institution. After two years at PCC I earned an associates in math and I was able to transfer to my dream school University of California Irvine, where I am currently studying Quantitative Economics. I've been tutoring and working with student since high school, first starting out by staying after school and tutoring students in need. From there, I took on more clients and amassed experience with the high school AP Calculus AB and BC curriculum.
